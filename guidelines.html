Paper Submission Guidelines

IEEE Format

Paper should be prepared strictly in IEEE format.

Submission Portal

Papers must be submitted through Microsoft CMT at: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com

The Microsoft CMT service is used for managing the peer-review process for this conference. This service is provided free by Microsoft, covering all expenses including Azure cloud services, software development, and support.

Paper Length

Preferred paper length is 6–7 pages. An additional charge of 50 USD per page will apply beyond this limit.

Plagiarism Policy

Plagiarism must be less than 10%, with no more than 5% from a single source.

AI Content Policy

AI-generated content is not allowed (NIL).
